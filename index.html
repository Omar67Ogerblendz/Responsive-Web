A Creeper is a tall, green, four-legged creature with a strange face and no arms. It is very quiet and does not make much noise as it moves around. When it gets close to a player, it starts to hiss like a burning fuse. After a few seconds, it explodes, damaging the player and destroying anything nearby.
A skeleton in Minecraft is a hostile mob that looks like a walking set of bones. It uses a bow and arrows to attack players from a distance. Skeletons usually spawn at night or in dark places like caves. When defeated, they can drop bones and arrows. If exposed to sunlight during the day, they start to burn unless they are wearing a helmet.
